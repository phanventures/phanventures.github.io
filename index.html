Who's Behind This

I'm Anh — born in Saigon, raised in Massachusetts, now building in Minnesota. Growing up as the kid of Vietnamese immigrants, I learned early that belonging isn't something that just happens. It's something you build, intentionally, for the people who don't already have it.

I'm a queer entrepreneur running multiple businesses alongside a full-time day job. Everything I build starts with the same question: does this make people feel like they belong?

My dream is for people to say, "That's where I found my people" — and then go build their own.
